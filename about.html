EasyTrans All In One Transport Companion App

How It Started

Surely, this year 2020 has been a blast. Who must have thought that there would come a virus in near future that would have a potential to knock entire world down? Sadly that has arrived and has infected many. Thanks to IBM HACKATHON who organized this hackathon that enabled us to think creatively and productively. EasyTrans is a smart Pocket Transport Companion App. It allows you to book transport online facilating contactless services using Online Payment & QR Scanning as a strategy. It is integrated with realtime temperature monitoring services to facilitate Temperature Control.

Ideas - Brainstorming

  • Developing an App/Web such that it can be incorporated with QR scanning technology and online booking of buses and metros as per the availability, this barcode would be scanned by Conductors/ Drivers or Officers on the station. [Web and App Coding]
  • QR Code will be used to identify individuals by linking their profile with it. Each QR code will also contain information about that particular Travel That is Destination, Source, Bus/Metro information.
  • Offline to online ticket availability ratio could also be calculated based on taking public surveys over the app and visualizing the data. [Analytical Way]
  • If a person’s temperature is found to be high (Positive), He/ She could be allocated seat under the quarantine zone (if exists).
  • App maintaining a track of all passengers especially those who were detected (Positive) by the app. History includes place of boarding & arrival, and passengers nearby. [Security-(Integrated with Covid-19 Tracker)]
  • Temperature sensing devices could be placed on the entrance/seats of buses and trains, to physically detect if a person is infected or having fever. [IOT]
  • A Donation checkbox on the payment section, which can be used to generate revenue by authority or can be contributed to PM Cares Fund.
  • Making it real time such that based on location data and how the people in that location are exposed or comfortable towards the usage of online apps and websites. As per that data, a ratio of offline to online ticket availability could be decided. [ML Way]
  • Includes approval options as per the route based on different zones allocated by government, positive person’s permit approval to travel etc.
  • Pre-booking of tickets with an option to cancel before a specific time to get a particular % of refund.

Solution Description

Developing an App/Web such that it can be incorporated with QR scanning technology and online booking of commonly used public transportation.

  • Since, it is essential to keep the track of the people that have traveled in the past. This app would be designed keeping that part in mind. Along with the ease for the public, this app will be designed to help admins as well in maintaining this essential details of keeping track of passengers, real time scheduling based on availability and timings.
  • Also, QR scanning incorporation will also be used to uniquely identify each passengers and this QR code will be linked to the user’s profile sharing all it’s travel details etc
  • Using Bluetooth proximity to check if passengers are maintaining social distancing can also aid the process of fighting covid-19 and a safe travelling.
